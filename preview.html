NYC Camp

A Week-long, Free Drupal Conference in NYC

March 23-29, 2015

This free Drupal conference is bringing together some of the most brightest minds in Open Source. This week-long event is focused on exposing attendees to the latest advances in our field and increasing contributions back to the community.

Join us in March to share your knowledge and experiences as well as make new friends!

Monday - Sunday

Sprints during the entire week!

Join contributors from around the globe to sprint on Drupal core and contrib. In-person sprints are the most effective way to tackle difficult code challenges and to get mentored on how to submit patches and work on issues.

Thursday

Trainings

Learn from the best Drupal trainers on a variety of topics. Opportunities will be available for all skill levels in multiple formats.

Friday

Summits

Discuss specific challenges and opportunities in various domain areas with your peers. Depending on the topic area of interest, summit formats can range from round-table discussions to highly structured forums with an agenda and presentations.

Saturday

Sessions & Plenaries

Participate in a variety of presentations on Drupal and related technologies. Enjoy thought-provoking discussions during our plenaries and when networking with your fellow attendees.
