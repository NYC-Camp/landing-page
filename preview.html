NYC Camp

A Week-long, Free Drupal Conference in NYC

March 23-29, 2015

NYC Camp is a free, week-long Drupal conference in New York City. We invite you to come learn from some of the brightest minds in Open Source, and expand your horizons. There will be numerous opportunities to contribute back to the community, so jump in!

Join us in March to share your knowledge and experiences as well as to make new friends.

Interested in volunteering or finding out more? Please sign up for our newsletter! :)

Monday - Sunday

Code Sprints during the entire week!

Join contributors from around the globe for a week-long sprint on Drupal core, with a special Sprint day on Sunday. In-person sprints are the most effective way to tackle difficult code challenges and to get mentored on how to submit patches and work on issues.

Thursday

Training Day

Learn from the best Drupal trainers on a variety of topics. Opportunities will be available for all skill levels in multiple formats.

Friday

Summit Day

Discuss specific challenges and opportunities in various domain areas with your peers. Depending on the topic area, summit formats can range from round-table discussions to highly structured forums with an agenda and presentations.

We have the following summits planned for 2015:

  • Apps & Start-Up
  • Commerce
  • DevOps
  • Drupal Core
  • Higher Ed
  • Media & Digital Publishing
  • Non-Profit & NGO
  • Security & Privacy and Training

Saturday

Sessions & Plenaries Day

Participate in a variety of presentations on Drupal and related technologies. Enjoy thought-provoking discussions during our plenaries and when networking with your fellow attendees.

Sunday

Sprint Day

Finish the conference by collaborating and applying your knowledge on our final Sprint Day.

Sprint Day will include workshops and plenty of mentored sprints. Here's a taste of what we're planning:

  • Community Tools Workshop - A workshop that will get you up and running with the basic tools that the Drupal community uses to tackle problems together.
  • Get Involved with Core Sprint - A mentored sprint designed especially for first-time contributors.

We will also have a number of focused sprints, including a D8 Core sprint and a Media sprint.
