NYC Camp

A Weeklong, Sprint-Centric Mini-DrupalCon in NYC

March 23-29, 2015

Monday - Sunday

Sprints

Join contributors from around the globe for a weeklong sprint on Drupal core and contrib. In person sprints the most effective way to tackle difficult code challenges and to get mentored on how to submit patches and work on issues.

Thursday

Trainings

Learn from the best Drupal trainers on a variety of topics. Opportunities will be available for all skill levels in multiple formats.

Friday

Summits

Discuss the specific challenges and opportunities in various domain areas with your peers. Depending on the topic area of interest, summit formats can range from roundtable discussions to highly structured forums with an agenda and presentations.

Saturday

Sessions & Plenaries

Participate in a variety of presentations on Drupal and related technologies. Also, enjoy thought-provoking discussions during our plenaries and by networking with your fellow attendees.
